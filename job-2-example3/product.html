Featured Plant

Monstera Deliciosa

Large, sculptural foliage for bright, leafy homes.

  • Light: Bright indirect
  • Water: Weekly, dry topsoil between watering
  • Toxicity: Toxic if pets ingest
  • Repotting: Every 18 months

Plant Parent Tips

Rotate your Monstera monthly so leaves grow evenly, and prune any brown edges gently.
