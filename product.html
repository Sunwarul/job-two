Featured Plant

Monstera Deliciosa

A bold, architectural plant with large split leaves that brings drama to any room.

  • Light: Bright indirect light
  • Water: Weekly, allow topsoil to dry
  • Toxicity: Toxic to pets if ingested
  • Repotting: Every 18 months for healthy growth

Plant Parent Tips

Keep Monstera near a north or east-facing window and mist its leaves when the air is dry.

Support the stem with a moss pole for upright growth and prune gently once a year.
