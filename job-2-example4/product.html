Monstera Deliciosa

A bold statement plant that thrives in bright indirect light.

  • Light: Bright indirect
  • Water: Weekly, keep soil slightly moist
  • Toxicity: Not safe for pets
  • Repotting: Every 18 months

Plant Parent Tips

Use a support pole for sturdy growth and avoid direct afternoon sun.
